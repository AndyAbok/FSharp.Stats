Calculation Examples for Matrices

Get sum of each row.

1: 
2: 

A
|> Matrix.Generic.enumerateRowWise (Seq.sum)

[10.0; 5.0; 12.0]

Create seq of vectors of matrix columns.

1: 
2: 

A
|> Matrix.mapiCols (fun i r -> r)

[vector [|1.0; 1.0; 2.0|]; vector [|7.0; 3.0; 9.0|]; vector [|2.0; 1.0; 1.0|]]
